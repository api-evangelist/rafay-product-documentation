Rafay has joined Cisco's Solutions Plus program, making the Rafay Platform purchasable directly alongside Cisco's AI infrastructure hardware, networking, and security stack. The partnership offers three software bundles - Foundation, Compute, and AI - enabling neocloud and sovereign AI providers to rapidly deliver GPU-as-a-Service and model inference capabilities. Cisco VP Samir Parikh cited the need for providers to quickly convert GPU capacity into competitive managed services as the key driver of the collaboration.
