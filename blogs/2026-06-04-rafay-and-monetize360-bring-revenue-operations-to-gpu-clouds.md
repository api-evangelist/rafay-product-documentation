Rafay and Monetize360 announced a direct integration enabling GPU cloud providers to convert metered infrastructure usage into automated billing and revenue workflows. Rafay tracks all GPU hours and token consumption through its orchestration platform, while Monetize360's quote-to-cash layer transforms that data into rate cards, committed usage models, and accounting system integrations. The partnership targets neoclouds and sovereign AI cloud operators who need to monetize GPU-as-a-Service offerings at scale.
